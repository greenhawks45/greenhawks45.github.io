Jonathan Jaques Run for Life

June 13, 2026

Come join Narbonne High School and Redondo Union High School as they perform a cadence at the annual Jonathan Jaques' Cahmpions Run for Life fundraiser/awareness event, also known as the Torchrun.

Each year the Jonathan Jaques Foundation supports children and families affected by childhood cancer and serious blood disorders. The Champions Run for Life is just one of the many ways that they fundraise and increase awareness for their research and support programs. The main event of the day is an Olympic-style relay race filled with uplifting moments as patients carry a torch, a symbol of hope for a cure, while they walk, run, wheel or are carried through a one-eighth-mile celebratory lap.

For NHS and RUHS students, they will be playing a cadence at the opening cerimonies and then later at the end of the event they will volunteer at the post-run carnival event by running booths and handing out prizes. If you would like to see how you can volunteer please reach out to us here!
