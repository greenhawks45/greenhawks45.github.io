Indoor Percussion Shows

Artemis

Goddess of the Moon & the Hunt

Scholastic Novice / Regional A

Show Duration: 5:00

$900 + Licensing Fees

A show about the Greek goddess Artemis, the goddess of the moon and the hunt. The concept is portrayed through elements that relate to the moon and hunting through song choice and samples in each part of the show.

This show will have ~$600 in additional fees required to license and perform the songs used in this composition. The licensing process is taken care of by us and the cost of each song's license will appear on separate lines when you receive your invoice.

Licensed Songs Required:
  • Running with the Wolves - AURORA
  • Artemis - Lindsey Stirling
Instrumentation:
    Battery
  • Snare
  • Tenor
  • Bass (3-5)
  • Cymbals
    • Front Ensemble
  • 2-3 Marimbas
  • Vibraphone
  • Xylo
  • Kit
